About Us

The Days for Girls club at Northwood High aims to raise awareness about period poverty and help support women with their menstrual needs. We raise funds to assemble menstrual kits for distribution to women in our community and around the world. We also discuss important and engaging topics in our club meetings, along with sharing club events.

Club Meetings: every other Monday - 2:00 to 2:30 PM
