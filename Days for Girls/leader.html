President

Snigdha Arabandi

Founded Club in 2020

I created this club because I felt that there was a need in our privileged community to raise awareness about period poverty. I have always been passionate about feminism and menstrual issues and thought that students at Northwood should be provided with the opportunity to help support women with their menstrual care needs through this initiative.
